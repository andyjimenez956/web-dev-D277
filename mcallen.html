South Texas Charm

McAllen is located in the southern tip of Texas, known for its warm climate, family friendly enviroment, and vibrant cross-border trade with Mexico.

McAllen City Overview

Population Approximately 143,000 Based on recent U.S. Census Estimates
Year Incorporated 1911 Officially becasme a city in Hidalgo County
Region Southern Texas (Rio Grande Valley) Near the U.S.- Mexico Border
Classification Urban Serves as a major economic hub for the Valley
